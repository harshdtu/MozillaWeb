About Us!

Prescription medicines home delivered to any pincode in India

We are an end to end healthcare service provider where the doctors can enter their prescriptions, patients can upload their prescriptions, their periodic vitals as well as order the regular medications through us (phone, web portal, mobile app, etc) and get it delivered to their doorstep, get awareness regarding their health conditions, prescription and doctor appointment reminders, etc.

Our Mission is to ensure that all people have access to high-quality medical care at all times through innovative technologies at affordable cost .

Our Vision is to have a seamless interaction and movement of data between all the principals of the health industry viz, govt, manufacturers, distributors, retailers, doctors and patients.
